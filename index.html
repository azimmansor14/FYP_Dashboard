Most In-Demand Sectors

1. Technology & Digital

Software developers, data analysts, and cybersecurity specialists remain highly sought after with 25% year-on-year growth in job postings.

2. Healthcare

Malaysia faces a shortage of 12,000 nurses and medical professionals as healthcare demands increase.

3. Green Economy

Renewable energy and sustainability jobs have grown by 40% since 2021, particularly in solar and EV sectors.

Emerging Workforce Trends

Hybrid Work Becoming Standard

72% of Malaysian companies now offer hybrid work arrangements, up from 35% pre-pandemic.

Skills Gap Challenge

58% of employers report difficulty finding candidates with required digital skills, especially in AI and cloud computing.

Gig Economy Growth

Freelancers now constitute 26% of Malaysia's workforce, with platforms like Grab and Shopee driving demand.

Regional Employment Highlights

Klang Valley

Accounts for 42% of all job postings, with strong demand in finance and professional services.

Penang

Electronics manufacturing hub seeing 18% increase in engineering roles.

Johor

Growing opportunities in logistics and construction linked to Singapore cross-border projects.
