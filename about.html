About

Mission

Malaysia Job Market Insights is a platform that offers a clear, visual understanding of the current employment landscape in Malaysia. By analyzing job data from leading job portals, we provides insights into hiring trends, top industries, and market performance. Our goal is to help job seekers, employers, and policy makers make informed decisions based on reliable data — not guesswork. Through our interactive dashboard, users can explore employment patterns and opportunities across Malaysia’s job market.

Dashboard

The dashboard was built using Power BI and structured into four section:

  • Overview: A summary of unemployment rate in Malaysia for the previous years.
  • Trend: Visualizations that show job demand and salary analysis across different sectors and location.
  • Analysis: Deep dives into the job market performance by measuring the Gross Domestic Product performance.
  • Prediction: Forecasting models that predict future job market using past trends and insights.
